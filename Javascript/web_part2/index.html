Pizza

Pizza is a popular and tasty food that originated in Italy. It is made with a flat bread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings like vegetables, chicken, or pepperoni. Pizza is loved all over the world because it is easy to customize and can be enjoyed by people of all ages. It is often eaten at parties, gatherings, or as a quick meal.
